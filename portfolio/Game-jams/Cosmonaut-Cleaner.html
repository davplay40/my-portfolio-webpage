Cosmonaut Cleaner

This was make doing the Global Game Jam 2025 at GCU despite the weather disruption. This game was made with a team of three people and only 48 hours to make a game with the theme of 'bubbles'.

This game is about Cleanse this brave explorer of the cosmos of all the gunk, dirt, and grime! Use the tools at hand to make them look fresh out of the factory.

We decided to use Unity as the team is comfortable with it, and we started talking about the theme, and we came up with cleaning a cosmonaut that came back from space. I made the soap bubble particle effect that spawns when you are cleaning and I added the music and sounds to the game.
