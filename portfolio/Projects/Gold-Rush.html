Gold Rush

I made this game at uni for the Network Game Programming model. We learnt how network works in general and in games. I made a simple multiplayer platformer game where you have to race to the top of a mountain.

I started with setting up Unity's Netcode getting it working. After that I added the player and got the movement and animation to sync with other players. Also added coins which adds to the player's score and sync it to other players. Then added enemies that move about and syncs and added a networked projectile so the player can shoot the enemies.
